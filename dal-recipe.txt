Ingredients:
  - 2 cups of water
  - 1 tsp cooking oil
  - 2 tsp cumin
  - 2 tsp mustard seeds
  - 1 dried red chilli
  - 3 tsp asofoetida powder
  - 1/2 cup moong dal
  
Directions:
  1.Wash the moong dal thoroughly and soak it in 250ml water and heat it on moderate flame
    till it cooks.
  2.In frying pan add oil,cumin,mustard,chilli and asofoetida powder and heat till mustard seeds 
    start popping
  3.Add this mixture to the precooked moongdal and add 1tsp turmeric and salt according to the taste 
    and heat for two minutes.
